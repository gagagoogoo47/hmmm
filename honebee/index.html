Honeybees

Honeybees are social insects that live in large colonies. They are important pollinators and also produce honey and beeswax.

Roles in the Hive

  • Queen: lays eggs.
  • Workers: gather food, build, and care for the hive.
  • Drones: mate with queens.

Why They Matter

They pollinate many crops and plants, helping nature and farming.

Threats

Pesticides, diseases, parasites, and loss of habitat endanger honeybees.

How to Help

Plant flowers, avoid harmful chemicals, support local beekeepers.
