Our Story

Located in the heart of Nagaland, Food Balance started with a simple mission: to provide nutritious, home-style meals that don't compromise on flavor. We believe that a balanced diet is the key to a happy life, whether you are craving local favorites or international classics.

Our Multi-Cuisine Menu

We take pride in our diverse menu that brings the best flavors of the world to Nagaland. At Food Balance, you can enjoy:

  • Indian: Rich curries, tandoori specialties, and traditional thalis.
  • Chinese: Authentic stir-fries, noodles, and dim sum favorites.
  • Italian: Freshly made pastas and classic pizzas.
  • Local Naga Flavors: Organic dishes sourced directly from local farmers.

Why Choose Us?

Whether you are a student looking for a quick lunch or a family seeking a cozy dinner spot, we offer a trendy and welcoming atmosphere. We focus on organic ingredients and also provide a wide range of vegan and vegetarian options to ensure everyone finds their perfect balance.
